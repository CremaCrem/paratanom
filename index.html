What is Paratanom?

ABOUT THE GAME

Paratanom is an augmented reality 2D farming top-down singleplayer game developed as a capstone project at Partido State University. It was designed to address the generational gap in the Philippine agricultural sector by making farming more engaging and accessible for younger generations.

The game combines gamification, augmented reality, and educational content to introduce users to sustainable agricultural practices in the Partido region of Camarines Sur, Philippines.

Players identify local crops through AR image recognition, simulate farming activities, manage virtual farms, complete educational challenges, and learn about the agricultural heritage of Partido.

What Makes It Special

KEY FEATURES

Where augmented reality meets cozy farming — learn about Partido's local crops through adventure, exploration, and hands-on gameplay.

AR TECHNOLOGY EDUCATIONAL YOLO v8n

AR CROP SCANNER

Tap the camera button and point your device at a real crop. Paratanom's YOLO-trained model detects it in real-time, then spawns an interactive 3D model right on your screen. Rotate it, inspect it, and learn everything about it — from planting tips to local uses — through reading or text-to-speech.

WHY IT MATTERS

Bridges the gap between real-world agriculture and digital learning — students see the actual crop and its 3D counterpart side by side, making knowledge stick.

GAMEPLAY COZY FARMING EDUCATIONAL

FARM & GROW

Buy seeds from the in-game NPC store — but only after you've scanned the real crop first. Plant, nurture, and harvest on your virtual farm, then process your produce into local delicacies and useful tools. The coconut alone can be cooked, eaten, processed, and crafted into multiple products.

WHY IT MATTERS

Gameplay fun meets real agricultural knowledge — players learn crop processing chains and local food culture through hands-on farm simulation.

COLLECTION EDUCATIONAL PROGRESS

CROP NOTEBOOK

Every crop you scan and grow gets logged into your personal notebook — your in-game encyclopedia of Partido's agriculture. Track your money, quests, achievements, and most importantly, review the crops you've collected. It's your go-to reference for refreshing everything you've learned.

WHY IT MATTERS

Acts as a personal agricultural reference — students can revisit crop information anytime, reinforcing long-term retention of what they've learned.

// UNDER THE HOOD

YOLO v8n DETECTION

Custom-trained on real crop images photographed by the team across Partido district. Real-time detection with confidence scoring for 11 local crop varieties.

BUILT WITH UNITY

Powered by Unity's Inference Engine for seamless ML model integration. The engine's mobile support and extensive game dev ecosystem made it the ideal choice.

Game Loop

HOW IT WORKS

A simple yet engaging loop that keeps you learning and growing.

1

SCAN

Point your camera at a real crop. The AR system identifies it using YOLOv8n and shows a 3D model.

2

COLLECT

Add discovered crops to your notebook. Learn about planting, maintenance, and pest management.

3

FARM

Grow crops on your virtual farm, manage resources, process produce, and complete challenges.

4

PROGRESS

Level up, unlock new content and story, take quizzes, and become a Partido farming expert!

The TAM evaluation yielded a Total Average Weighted Mean of 4.57, signifying "Strongly Agree" interpretation and reflecting robust user acceptance among the 144 Agribusiness students. Students found the game useful, easy to navigate, engaging, and intended to continue using it.

Knowledge Retention

High Engagement
